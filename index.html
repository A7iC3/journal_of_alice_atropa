About me

I am a disabled transgender woman trying to survive in this world, and this is my personal website! I love doing whatever I can to make the people around me happy, and being able to create things for people by doing the things I love is something I really enjoy.

I am not a fan of formality; I prefer to conduct myself with people as I am as a person. So, while I may not necessarily be very "business-like," I am genuine, caring, and strive to do my best. If you have a vision that I can help create with you, then I would be thrilled to do the best I can!

Background

I am an avid video game enjoyer, especially within the RPG genres. I have been playing video games for the vast majority of my life, and I deeply want to create my own one day. I especially love MMOs, as they really helped me socialize and make friends when I didn't have much opportunity to in real life.

Getting to meet so many people from around the world was also a wonderful experience, and I made many friends in several countries. Some friends even with whom we didn't share a language, and I made the effort to reach out to help people and socialize with those who didn't know English by using Google Translate. The ability to play a game you enjoy with so many people and meet others within it was such a magical experience for me.

That truly instilled the desire to create my own world where many people can adventure and explore together. It's truly a shame how much monetization and other less than savory practices have invaded the video game sphere. While I don't expect anything to come from my own choices alone, I at least want to give back to the community a world that is truly for the players—one that is purely just for their enjoyment and to provide that same safe, comfortable space I was given when I was younger.
